Skill [Apex Instinct]
[Class Skill]

The bearer instinctively perceives creatures below their combined effective level with exceptional range and clarity. Creatures at or above the bearer's effective level lie beyond the reach of this sense and cannot be detected through it. The internal mana structure of mana-dense creatures may be perceived at moderate range. Faint signatures can be distinguished by their patterns of movement. Lesser creatures can be tracked individually at close range, and numerous signatures may be followed at once. The sense remains active during sleep.
