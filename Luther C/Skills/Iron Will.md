Skill [Iron Will]
[Skill] (Superseded)

Panic, fear, and coercion resistance significantly increased. The bearer may now exert conscious resistance against compulsions acting on the mind, including those that cannot be removed. Compulsion does not vanish; the will to act against it is strengthened.

Evolved from [[Stone Mind]]. Merged into [[Adamant]] alongside [[Mantle]] and [[Anchored]].
