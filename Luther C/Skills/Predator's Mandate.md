**Type:** Class Skill
**Current Holder:** [[Luther Culvert]]
**Level:** 8

---

Upon fully consuming a target, the bearer may attempt to absorb one intrinsic aspect and one gifted aspect simultaneously. Specific aspects may be targeted during extraction without additional burden. Failed extractions still contribute to attribute growth. Large-scale consumption may be combined toward a single extraction attempt. Directed extractions are more likely to succeed, and near-successes may yield fragments of the desired aspect. Enchantments and other non-living mana structures may be studied through repeated consumption and eventually reproduced as skills.

---

**Origin:** Evolved from [[Predator's Claim]] at Level 6.
