**Type:** Class Skill
**Current Holder:** [[Luther Culvert]]
**Granted By:** [[Murtha, Goddess of the Second Thread]]
**Level:** 1

---

The bearer's resistance to mental, magical, and environmental pressure is massively increased. Stress accumulation is reduced, shock recovery is accelerated, compulsions are easier to notice, and passive mana absorption from high-density environments is improved. Active — can be toggled.

---

**Origin:** Combined from [[Iron Will]], [[Mantle]], and [[Anchored]]. [[Murtha, Goddess of the Second Thread|Murtha]] waived the combination quest in exchange for daily prayer and a year of weekly visits to a priest of the Second Thread upon leaving the Deeproot.
