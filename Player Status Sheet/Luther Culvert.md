CLASS SKILLS — Devourer

[[Adamant]] (Level 1) — The [Devourer] has resistance to mental, magical, and environmental pressure massively increased. Stress accumulation reduced, shock recovery accelerated, compulsions are easier to notice, and passive mana absorption from high-density environments improved. Active — can be toggled.

[[Murtha, Goddess of the Second Thread]]

[[Gorge]] (Level 1) — The [Devourer] can consume organic matter for use toward immediate physical restoration.

[[Grinding Halt]] (Level 4) — Force required to displace, reposition, or halt the [Devourer] substantially increased.

(Notes from levels) (The [Devourer] maintains stability even while off-balance.)

[[Predator's Mandate]] (Level 9) — Upon fully consuming a target, the [Devourer] may attempt to absorb one intrinsic aspect and one gifted aspect simultaneously.

(Notes from levels) (Specific aspects may be targeted during extraction without additional burden. Failed extractions still contribute to attribute growth. Large-scale consumption may be combined toward a single extraction attempt. Directed extractions are more likely to succeed, and near-successes may yield fragments of the desired aspect. Enchantments and other non-living mana structures may be studied through repeated consumption and eventually reproduced as skills. Extraction against resistant trait overlap may be forced through volume and sustained intent.)

[[Unfathomable Gluttony]] (Level 10) — All consumed matter converts toward attribute growth with increased efficiency. Hunger cannot be removed. Mana-rich biological matter yields substantially greater growth. Dense and resistant matter is broken down with minimal loss. Non-biological substances including worked materials and enchanted objects may be consumed and converted. Consumption speed increased.

[[Apex Instinct]] (Level 7) — The [Devourer] instinctively perceives creatures below their combined effective level with exceptional range and clarity. Creatures at or above the [Devourer's] effective level lie beyond the reach of this sense and cannot be detected through it.

(Notes from levels) (The internal mana structure of mana-dense creatures may be perceived at moderate range. Faint signatures can be distinguished by their patterns of movement. Lesser creatures can be tracked individually at close range, and numerous signatures may be followed at once. The sense remains active during sleep.)

[TRAITS]

[[Unyielding Frame]] — Luther's bones, skin, and blood vessels reinforce. Blunt force is dispersed through increased bone density. Cutting and piercing injuries are resisted through skin hardening. Blood loss from open wounds is actively suppressed. Luther's body adapts rapidly to repeated physical stress. Bone, skin, and circulatory tissue continue to strengthen through mana absorption, exertion, and trauma.

(Current integration: early.)

[[Ravenous Constitution]] — Luther can digest consumed matter with extreme efficiency. Mana-dense material is actively broken down and integrated into his body. Physical volume limitations are reduced. Spoiled, toxic, or uncooked material causes no harm when consumed. Luther can consume and process large quantities of matter at an accelerated rate.

[[Slow Mend]] (Level 13) — Luther passively regenerates damaged tissue. Superficial wounds close rapidly, while moderate injuries heal at a steady pace. Deep tissue and structural damage are continuously repaired over time.

(Notes from levels) (Mana can be consciously directed to influence recovery. Neurological strain and repetitive stress injuries are gradually restored.)

[[Serrated Mandible]] — Luther's bite and unarmed contact possess a passive shearing quality. His teeth and nails develop microscopic serrations through continued mana absorption, increasing their ability to cut and tear.

Current shearing intensity: minimal.

[[Tight Jaw]] — Luther's bite force is significantly increased. Jaw musculature develops beyond normal physical limits. Lockjaw reflex approaching threshold.

[[Scent Nullification]] — Luther's natural scent is suppressed. Ambient odors are absorbed and dispersed more effectively, reducing his olfactory signature toward that of his surroundings.

Current suppression: moderate.

[[Loping Gait]] — Luther moves with increased cardiovascular and muscular efficiency during sustained travel. Running requires less stamina, and stride mechanics gradually optimize themselves through use.

Current efficiency: moderate.

[[Rending Claws]] — Luther's bite attacks and unarmed strikes possess a tearing quality. Wounds inflicted are more difficult to close naturally.

Current intensity: minimal.

[[Fey Mana Affinity]] — Luther possesses an increased internal mana capacity and draws ambient mana more readily than normal.

[[War Hide]] — Luther's outer tissue toughens under repeated combat stress, reducing damage from physical strikes over sustained engagements.

Current intensity: early.

[[Crushing Weight]] — Luther's mass and density increase the impact force of downward and overhead strikes. Ground beneath Luther compresses under sustained pressure.

Current intensity: minimal.

[[Mana Saturation]] — Luther's body passively absorbs and retains ambient mana at high density. Internal mana concentration remains elevated even in low-mana environments.

Current saturation: minimal.

[[Mana Shell]] — Luther's outer tissue reinforces itself through sustained mana absorption, increasing in density and hardness proportional to mana exposure over time.

Current density: minimal.

[[Enhanced Magic]] — Luther's mana interacts more potently with active skills. Abilities driven by mana produce stronger results relative to the energy invested.

Current intensity: minimal.
