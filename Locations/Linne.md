A village in [[The Deeproot]], located south of the goblin staging ground destroyed by [[Caedmon's Group]] on [[Day 10]]. Defended by a wooden palisade surrounding a central square. Has a tavern called the Soaked Log.

Came under assault by roughly 300 goblin variants on [[Day 11]] (Greenmonth 14th). South palisade destroyed. Defenders compressed into a final ring before [[Luther Culvert|Luther]] and [[Cademon Vale|Caedmon]]'s group intervened and destroyed the attacking force.

A goblin encampment approximately four miles southeast of Linne contained roughly 600 goblin variants launching raids on the village. Destroyed by Luther solo on [[Day 12]].

[[Caedmon's Group]] based here through [[Day 12]]–[[Day 14|14]]. [[Bevan Roe|Bevan]] rebuilt the destroyed southern palisade section during the stay. The group departed south for [[Harren's Mill]] on [[Day 14]] (Greenmonth 17th).

Notable residents: [[Teysa Vael]] ([Verdant Warden] Level 26, Rare, age 15), [[Cael Harrick]] ([Stoneshaper] Level 24, age 14). Craftspeople: Dorrit (leatherworker — made [[Luther Culvert|Luther]]'s holster and bandoliers), Fesser (carpenter — made the munition vial racks).

Southern route from Linne: [[Harren's Mill]] (~60 miles south), [[Cairnhollow]] (~180 miles south).
