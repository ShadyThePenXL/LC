A walled stone settlement in [[The Deeproot]], roughly 60 miles south of [[Linne]] on the southern route toward [[Cairnhollow]] and the deeper forest. Harren's Mill sits about 10 miles short of the first mana density ring — the last town before the ambient saturation steps up.

### The Siege (Greenmonth 17th–18th)

When [[Caedmon's Group]] arrived on [[Greenmonth 17th]], the town had been under siege four days by roughly 5,000 goblin variants drawing fresh troops from the first ring just south. Stone walls held; provisions for two more weeks. Defended by a garrison of ~112 fighters under [[Edric Holm]] ([Knight] Level 38), with [[Maren Ashell]] ([Sentinel] Level 31) as wall-watch captain, and four town [[Harren's Mill Mages|[Mages]]] (Carys, Tomas, Aldric, Fenn) holding the wards. Eight goblin shamans ran ward-suppressed pressure on the southeast wall.

[[Luther Culvert|Luther]] and the group broke the siege over two days — running the gauntlet to breach the northeast wall, then a dusk sortie that destroyed the shaman line and broke the southeast pressure, followed by Luther's solo night strike on the reinforced southeast line. See [[Greenmonth 17th]] and [[Greenmonth 18th]].
