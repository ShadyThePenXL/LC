Monster [Bugbear]

Stats per level: +3 Str, +3 Dex

Fast and strong for their size. Bugbears operate as perimeter guards, trackers, and ambush units. They are quieter than their build suggests and difficult to detect by scent.

Lifecycle: Adult at 6 months. Lifespan ~20 years. 1/5 human.

Traits: [[Scent Nullification]]
