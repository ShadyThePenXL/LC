Monster [Goblin Brute]

Stats per level: +2 Str, +2 End

Heavy goblin variant built for direct combat. Brutes are physically powerful and can reach levels where they pose a serious threat through raw force alone. Elite specimens may develop additional skills.

Lifecycle: Adult at 1 month. Lifespan ~10 years. 1/5 human.
