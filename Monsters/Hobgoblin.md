Monster [Hobgoblin]

Stats per level: +1 Str, +1 Dex, +1 End

Larger and more durable than goblins. Hobgoblins fill roles requiring endurance and reliability — supply guards, trackers, and sustained patrols. Their bone structure is denser than standard goblin variants.

Lifecycle: Adult at 3 weeks. Lifespan ~15 years. 1/8 human.

Traits: [[Iron Marrow]], [[Tight Jaw]]
