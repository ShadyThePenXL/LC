Monster [Mana Warden]

Stats per level: +10 Str, +10 End, +10 Int, +10 Kno

Territorial guardians native to [[The Deeproot]]. Wardens feed on titan tree sap and defend sap-producing trees and the surrounding area. They are among the most dangerous things in the forest. Their bodies are heavily reinforced and they regenerate damage continuously.

Lifecycle: Adult at 100 years. Lifespan unknown. Requires approximately 15 times more experience than a human to level.

Traits: [[Deep Regeneration]], [[Living Bulwark]], [[Mana Saturation]], [[Rooted Stability]], [[Seismic Awareness]], [[Bark Plating]]
