HARREN'S MILL MAGES

The four [Mages] who held [[Harren's Mill]]'s wards through the siege. Exhausted from four days of sustained warding by the time [[Caedmon's Group]] arrived on [[Greenmonth 17th]].

**Carys** — Old, grey-haired, staff. The most experienced. Held the wards on the southeast wall for four days without breaking. Hit the ogre with a sustained force beam during the dusk sortie.

**Tomas** — Age 19. Shaking hands, exhausted. Held wards alongside Carys. Heard Luther eating on the southeast field at night and went back down the stairs.

**Aldric & Fenn** — The town's other two [Mages]. Contributed to wall defense during the sortie. Less individually detailed.
