**Thornwall** is under full [[Goblin|goblin]] siege — every city walled, every village palisaded, all territory surrounded by hordes. [[The Deeproot]] is a massive forest within its borders, trees growing over a thousand feet tall, thick with mana. The kingdom's military sent twenty-five patrol groups into the forest to deal with the threat. Most are confirmed destroyed or unaccounted for. [[Luther Culvert|Luther]]'s group is the last one confirmed still operating.
