Creature [Deeproot Siphon Turtle]

Small freshwater turtles found near mana-saturated water sources throughout [[The Deeproot]]. Roughly the size of a dinner plate. Their shells are smooth and faintly luminescent from prolonged mana absorption. They congregate in large numbers around pools, streams, and condensation runoffs beneath titan roots, drinking mana-infused water constantly. A group of ten can visibly lower the mana density of a small pool within hours.

Their flesh and shell are mana-dense relative to their size. Not aggressive. They retract into their shells when threatened and are too small and slow to pose any danger.

Traits: [[Mana Saturation]], [[Mana Shell]]
