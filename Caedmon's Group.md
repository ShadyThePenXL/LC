**Original Composition (15 members):**

- [[Cademon Vale]] — [Hunter], Level 34

- [[Bevan Roe]] — [Trapper], Level 22
- Pell — class and level unrecorded. Killed in a goblin engagement during the first week.
- Dobs— class and level unrecorded. Killed in a goblin engagement during the first week.

---

### Operational History

**Deployment:** Group 16 entered [[The Deeproot]] from the northern tree line alongside all other groups. Assigned to the northern interior sector.

**First Two Weeks:** The group conducted patrols and attacked small goblin camps. They lost twelve members across multiple engagements and hazards. Caedmon pulled the survivors back to a concealed position he designated Vault Camp — a defensible location used as a base of operations.

**Greenmonth 4th (Day 1):** [[Luther Culvert]] arrived in [[The Deeproot]] via divine transport from [[Andro, God of Borders and Passages|Andro]]. Caedmon encountered Luther after the latter cleared a goblin camp solo. They partnered — Luther would receive all corpses from the group's kills in exchange for fighting alongside them.

**Greenmonth 5th–10th (Days 2–7):** Luther's presence changed the group's operational capacity. His consumption-based growth accelerated rapidly. The group destroyed multiple goblin camps, and Luther hunted independently — killing [[Blackshell Ant|blackshell ants]], [[Deeproot Stone Lizard|stone lizards]], [[Bugbear|bugbears]], [[Hobgoblin|hobgoblins]], [[Worg|worgs]], and a [[Deeproot Shadow Cat]]. Luther scouted the goblin staging ground alone and began harassing its perimeter.

**Greenmonth 11th (Day 8):** The group traveled 100 miles northeast to a rendezvous point. Caedmon revealed there were no reinforcements — the rendezvous was a supply drop only. [[Trieht Blasterhubt]] appeared via projection and delivered supplies and the Mission Severance briefing: proceed 500 miles south to the center of [[The Deeproot]], locate and destroy a mana anomaly under the control of a drow [Sorcerer] named Veleth. No reinforcements. No rescue.

**Greenmonth 12th (Day 9):** The group assaulted the goblin staging ground on two fronts. Luther struck the western side. Caedmon's team hit the east with trap corridors set by [[Bevan Roe|Bevan]] and [[Toller Finch|Toller]]. Luther freed three prisoners from other expedition groups — [[Sable Mott]] (Group 9), [[Wyn Drace]] (Group 9), and [[Pol Harren]] (Group 5). Luther killed a Fey [Witch] named Ryn and obtained intelligence about Veleth and a drow [Mage] named Thalrun (Level 47) who commands the staging ground.
