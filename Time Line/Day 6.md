Day 6 — Colony Hunting and Staging Ground Reconnaissance (Greenmonth 9th)

In the mornign Luther killed another Queen (Level 3,891,407) of the first blackshell colony, feeding Saturated Chitin into Dense Hide. The quest remained at 1/10 as the queen was not unique. 

Cademon's group made a plan to go to the meet up spot 100 miles away which Cademon indicated they would get reinforcements from, though only Cademon knew that they were only getting supplies and new orders teleported in. 

Luther then went off alone and scouted the staging ground, using Apex Instinct to count numbers of forces and stalk around. He assassinated a Level 67 perimeter bugbear, acquiring Scent Nullification. Predator's Mandate leveled to 7. He ambushed the Level 71 resupply runner. before sleeping waiting for something else to show up.
