Day 1 — Death and Arrival (June 6, 2026 → Greenmonth 4th, Year 894)

[[Luther Culvert]] died in Pittsburgh An MMA fighter since the age of 12 transitioning into powerlifting for the last four years died while attempting an 800 lb. squat at age 36. 

His soul was pulled into [[Ardenvast]]. The System granted him the Outerworlder (Legendary) title. 

[[Murtha, Goddess of the Second Thread|Murtha]] authored his arrival record and granted him Anchored. Luther picked a class named [Endurer] a defensive class during class selection, just before [[Andro, God of Borders and Passages|Andro]] teleported him to the [[The Deeproot]] in [[Thornwall]] a massive forest with trees which grow thick and tall some over a thousand feet pulling mana from the ground and leaving the forest thick with it. 

 He granted him [[Andro, God of Borders and Passages|Iron Skin]]. (The kingdom of [[Thornwall]] is under a goblin siege all cities walled everything closed off all their land surrounded by hordes of goblins.)
 
 Luther killed a creature out of hunger and ate it raw this prompted [[Greel, God of Greed and Starvation|Greel]] to forcibly grant him [[Greel, God of Greed and Starvation|Unfathomable Gluttony]] and [[Greel, God of Greed and Starvation|Ravenous Constitution.]] While giving him a General Skill point which he used on [Combat Bludgeoning]
 
 Luther killed various creatures and then cleared a goblin camp, killing goblins. He had a kill on a brute stolen as a man showed up. The man turned out to be 
 Caedmon Vale A (Level 34 Hunter). 
 
 Caedmon and Luther partnered up and agreed Luther would get all corpses of anything that Caedmon and his group killed . Luther resisted his hunger, evolving Stone Mind into Iron Will. They traveled to Cademon's Vault Camp, meeting Toller Finch (Level 17 Scout) and Bevan Roe (Level 22 Trapper).  Bevan was unable to walk properly as his leg had been poisoned via a pitfall trap he fell into.
 
 Luther's consumption resulted in him leveling to level 5 where The System forced a class evolution. Luther fought the pull of a legendary class called [The Maw] leveling Iron Will to 23 in the process, and chose Devourer. He selected a class skill at level 5 called [Mantle]. With his evolution to Devourer he gained the class skill [Pedator's Claim]
