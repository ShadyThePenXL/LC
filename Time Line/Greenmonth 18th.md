Greenmonth 18th — Solo Strike on the Southeast Line

~12:50 AM — Against [[Cademon Vale|Caedmon]]'s advice, [[Luther Culvert|Luther]] crawled out through no man's land toward the reinforced southeast goblin line (~1,000 strong, a fresh ogre, no shamans). [[Muffle]] leveled 1 → 3 during the crawl (genuine stress use). He was nearly detected — a hobgoblin sentry stared at his position for ten seconds, then turned away.

At ~150 yards out Luther abandoned stealth and charged at full sprint with continuous combustion snaps, breaking into the ranks. The reinforced first-ring troops did not break — they formed shield walls and boxed him in. Luther planted his feet and spent 12 seconds building an omnidirectional shockwave (1 full [[Mana Well]] + 3/4 refill), tanking hits during construction — the rib cut widened, his shoulder was struck, his back raked, his calf cut, spear thrusts into armor gaps. [[Unyielding Frame]], [[War Hide]], and the chimera leather prevented major damage.

He released a horizontal disc of concussive force at ground level. The goblin brace failed completely. ~100+ kills, hundreds more scattered — shield walls are useless against omnidirectional force. The field flattened in a 100-yard radius.

The ogre (Level 221) braced and weathered the shockwave. Luther engaged it in melee with the mithril mace — the mace dented but couldn't break its dense hide, and the ogre's backhand reopened his rib cut and cracked something in his side. Luther switched to combustion: snapped to ignite the ambient mana around the ogre, then poured a sustained flamethrower on top. The dual-source fire overwhelmed it. Ogre killed.

### The First Ring

For fun, Luther sprinted 10 miles south to the first mana density ring and hit the transition at full speed. Mana sickness from rushing through it — vision blur, pressure behind the eyes, joint pain. [[Adamant]] engaged immediately (resistance to environmental pressure, improved high-density absorption) and reduced it to manageable discomfort. His Mana Well flooded to capacity in under 2 seconds.

The three escaped shamans ambushed him at the ring threshold with a coordinated mana bolt — three converging streams of compressed raw mana — that landed clean and cracked his sternum through Adamant and the chimera leather. Luther retaliated with [[Combustion]]: at first-ring density his fire was dramatically more powerful, the ambient mana detonating on ignition. Two shamans were vaporized instantly; the third caught the flamethrower. All three killed and consumed.

Combustion in the first ring is qualitatively different from anything Luther had used before.

### Key Discoveries

- Combustion can be used for propulsion (rocket-jump — combustion under the boots). Combustion is not inherently burning; Luther's magic covers the entire process of combustion.
- Air-pressure shockwaves are possible through understanding of particle physics.
- Omnidirectional shockwaves defeat surrounding forces — shield walls are useless against them.
- The glass pistol is confirmed effective in combat (enhanced rounds with Mana Well).
- [[Gorge]] was revealed to Caedmon, who understood the implication immediately.
