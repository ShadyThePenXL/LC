Day 8 — Rendezvous and Resupply (Greenmonth 11th)

The group departed Vault Camp before dawn. Caedmon carried Bevan on his back. Toller ran point ahead. Luther kept pace. They ran 100 miles northeast Arrived at the rendezvous point a shallow cave under a titan root at approximately 11:47 AM.

Caedmon revealed there are no reinforcements  the rendezvous is a supply drop only, teleported in. Toller and Bevan reacted poorly to learning Caedmon had withheld this. Caedmon defended the decision: he needed them running forward, not arguing.

Trieht Blasterhubt (Level 228 [Archmage], elf, aged 971, Thornwall Military Strategic Command) appeared via projection. He delivered 15 crates of supplies. 

He identified Luther immediately via high-level inspection — Outerworlder, [Devourer], Greel's mark, Murtha's thread, Earth origin. He noted that every Outerworlder from Earth he has encountered has been immensely powerful, always god-touched, and "different." He expressed concern about Greel's hunger but wants to speak with Luther after the mission is complete. He confirmed all other Deeproot expedition teams (15 total) are presumed destroyed. Group 16 is the last team operating in the forest.

Supplies were distributed. Notable items include Hollis Ferrow master-smith armor in chimera leather, Mithril Steel alloy weapons, archmage-worked Edgekeen arrows, a silver-inlaid shortsword forged specifically against a [Mage]'s ward-skin, anti-mage countermeasures (Dispel Chalk, Null Sand, Counter-resonance bell), Whisperweave cloaks, 

Luther claimed: heavy mace (Mithril Steel alloy), boots, full chimera leather armor set, Whisperweave cloak, two daggers, one long knife, one Mithril-coated axe, one Mithril-coated saw, Dispel Chalk, standard field supplies.

Mission briefing received via sealed orders (Mission Designation: "Severance"): Proceed to the center of the Deeproot (500 miles south of rendezvous point). Locate a severe mana anomaly — a dome-like barrier trapping all mana within the forest. Intelligence suggests significant goblin presence near the center under the control of a high-level classed [Mage] and [Warrior] duo. A ritual is believed to be underway. Destroy the goblin force, eliminate the [Mage], terminate the ritual. No reinforcements. No rescue. No further supplies. Timeline: four to six weeks.

Luther traveled 10 miles east of the rendezvous point and harvested raw sap from a titan tree using his Mithril-coated axe. He filled five waterskins (25 liters of sap decently infused with mana) for transport. He consumed three additional waterskins of raw sap, gaining stats. A Mana Warden signature approached the open sap wound and Luther departed, returning to the rendezvous cave with five full skins.

Luther consumed some of the whisper weave cloaks in an attempt to evolve Mandate, and he succeeded leveling the skill to level 8 and gaining [Muffle]

Luther proposed that the group attack the goblin staging ground using harassment tactics and Cademon agreed because if they can’t kill whats in there they’d have no chance in the deeper parts of the forest also it’s a good way to Level.

They traveled the 70 miles south.
