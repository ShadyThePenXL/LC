**Greenmonth 12th — Staging Ground Assault**

[[Toller Finch|Toller]] scouted the staging ground at dawn. Guard patterns had shifted from [[Luther Culvert|Luther]]'s earlier kills. [[Bevan Roe|Bevan]] and [[Cademon Vale|Cademon]] and Toller spent the day setting trap corridors along the eastern areas surrounding the base. 

At nightfall the group hit the staging ground on two fronts. Caedmon's team struck the eastern perimeter first. Luther waited ten minutes, burned a scrying ticket, and walked through the western gate after killing the lone guard.

Luther moved through the camp openly, killing [[Goblin|goblins]], [[Hobgoblin|hobgoblins]], [[Orc|orcs]], a [[Bugbear|bugbear]], and an [[Ogre|ogre]]. [[Stagger Shout]] broke through its Level 5 soft cap during the ogre fight. He found three [[Thornwall]] prisoners in pens near the [[Worg|worg]] kennels — [[Sable Mott]], [[Wyn Drace]], and [[Pol Harren]]. Luther freed them, armed them from his own gear temporarily, gave them a healing potion and titan sap, and directed them to hold the western gate.

Luther pushed into the camp's northern section and encountered Ryn, a Pale Skinned Fey [Witch] at Level 31. Ryn revealed critical intelligence for some reason: the true target at the center of [[The Deeproot]] is a drow [Sorcerer] named Veleth, Veleth's level is well beyond 50 though not directly stated. The staging ground is run by a drow [Mage] though what class he actually is was not said, named Thalrun (Level 47), who had left the camp east roughly two hours before Luther arrived. Ryn described the camp as inventory, not her people — her allegiance to Veleth appeared transactional rather than loyal.

Luther killed Ryn after a short fight — [[Stagger Shout]] disrupted her concentration, Dispel Chalk broke her ward, and her low physical stats couldn't survive melee range. Luther took her satchel and carried her body out through the western gate with the three freed prisoners.

The group linked up east of the camp. Luther shared the intelligence about Veleth. Caedmon's team had killed about a hundred on their side and withdrawn cleanly. 

Luther soon left back to the west gate as they would be attacking again in about an hour, and consumed Ryn and acquired a skill called [[Root Command]] (Level 3) as a stolen skill and [[Fey Mana Affinity]] as a trait. He reached Level 12. 

Luther opened Ryn's satchel and found potions which weren't of use. 

[[Cademon Vale|Caedmon]] outfitted the three freed prisoners — [[Sable Mott]], [[Wyn Drace]], and [[Pol Harren]] — from the [[Mission Severance Supply List|Mission Severance supplies]]. Full chimera leather armor, enchanted boots, Whisperweave cloaks, weapons suited to their classes. Sable received the field surgeon's kit, healing potions, and specialist medical supplies as the group's only [Cleric of Solenne]. Wyn got a shortsword and throwing axes to back up his glass. Pol got a longsword — no shield in the supply drop despite his [[Shield Brace]] skill. Caedmon put the high-grade healing potions and Sevenfold Salve under Sable's authority. 

Luther spent an hour practicing [[Root Command]] in [[The Deeproot]]. He learned the massive titan roots are too mana-saturated for Level 3 to affect, but thin undergrowth and fresh growth can be marginally manipulated within roughly ten feet. The Deeproot's high-density environment allowed for easy use, but he ran out of time to practice. [[Root Command]] leveled to 4.

Luther hit the camp a second time. He battered through a reinforced western barricade and carved through the camp setting fires with stolen torches. The western half of the staging ground burned. Luther killed numerous [[Goblin|goblins]], [[Hobgoblin|hobgoblins]], [[Orc|orcs]], and [[Bugbear|bugbears]] across both runs. Caedmon's team struck the eastern corridors again during the chaos. The clock ticked over to midnight.
