Day 2— Sharing Information and Lizard Hunting

[[Luther Culvert|Luther]] showed some of his skills to the group asking questions about them. [[Cademon Vale|Caedmon]] explained [[Greel, God of Greed and Starvation|Greel]] is unaligned and at war with [[Solenne, Goddess of the Radiant Court|Solenne]], warning that those with Greel's gifts usually go feral or are destroyed.

Luther purchased [[Hide Status]] (Level 1) from the general skill list. He hunted [[Deeproot Stone Lizard|Deeproot Stone Lizards]] for hours, killing many and using [[Predator's Claim]] 30 times to acquire and level [[Slow Mend]] to Level 8.
