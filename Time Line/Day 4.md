Day 4 — Ant Farming and Goblin Slavery (Greenmonth 7th)

Luther hunted blackshell ants. He excavated a colony, beginning progress on Serrated Mandible. He captured five goblins , utilizing them as living fodder in the ant pit before eating them.

Caedmon and Toller reported on a permanent goblin staging ground run by a drow [Mage] of some sort , containing thousands of goblins, hobgoblins, Goblin brutes orcs, ogres, bugbears, worgs, wolves, Goblin [Shamans] , and Fey (classed). 

Luther trained Stagger Shout on Bevan for two hours, leveling it to 4.
