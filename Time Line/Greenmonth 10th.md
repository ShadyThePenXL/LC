Greenmonth 10th — Tracker Response and Combat

Three trackers who were investigating the missing [[Bugbear|bugbears]]. [[Luther Culvert|Luther]] defeated them, acquiring [[Iron Marrow]] from a [[Hobgoblin|hobgoblin]], [[Tight Jaw]] from another, and [[Loping Gait]] from a [[Worg]].

Luther looped to the opposite side of the goblin staging ground, roughly a mile out. He baited and killed a few [[Bugbear|bugbears]]. He killed a [[Hobgoblin|hobgoblin]] supply guard which was guarding a forward supply cache. Luther took all eight bundles back to Vault Camp.

Luther drank the lamp oil. [[Predator's Mandate]] did not fire — rendered plant fat carries no System-recognized aspects. [[Unfathomable Gluttony|Gluttony]] processed it for negligible growth.

He finds [[Cademon Vale|Cademon]] and learns more about monsters and how their growth works. And then he asks how to combine skills.

He learns and then combines. [[Iron Marrow]], [[Dense Hide]], and [[Clotting]] into [[Unyielding Frame]] after a quick quest. Following this he combines [[Iron Will]], [[Mantle]], and [[Anchored]] into [[Adamant]]. [[Murtha, Goddess of the Second Thread|Murtha]] skips the quest and in return Luther will pray to her daily for a year and when leaving the forest will be required to just visit one of her priests.

Luther ventured southeast for roughly thirty minutes at full sprint so about 30 miles. He tracked a [[Goblin|goblin]] patrol heading west, following them to a forward camp which he took out. The camp was led by an [[Orc|orc]].

Which he ate the orc, directing [[Predator's Mandate]] toward [[War Hide]]. The trait fed into [[Unyielding Frame]] instead.

Luther continued southeast looking for stronger prey. He found a [[Deeproot Shadow Cat]]. He eventually killed and ate it. Acquired [[Rending Claws]] trait.

Skills leveled to [[Combat Bludgeoning]] 6→7, [[Grinding Halt]] 2→3, [[Low Light Vision]] 1→2. [[Stagger Shout]] pressing against Level 5 soft cap but has not broken through. Luther returned to the vault and slept.
