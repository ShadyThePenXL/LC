Greenmonth 17th.

7:15 AM — The group departed [[Linne]] by the south gate at full composition: [[Cademon Vale|Caedmon]], [[Toller Finch|Toller]], [[Bevan Roe|Bevan]], [[Luther Culvert|Luther]], [[Sable Mott|Sable]], [[Wyn Drace|Wyn]], [[Pol Harren|Pol]]. All supplied. Ordnance distributed — a bandolier each to Caedmon and Bevan, vial racks to Bevan, Wyn carrying spare rounds and replacement grenades. Bevan had test-rigged three land-mine assemblies on [[Greenmonth 16th]], all successful.

Over the two days at Linne: Bevan rebuilt the village's southern palisade section; Pol drilled under Caedmon and improved noticeably; Sable restocked medical supplies; Toller scouted the first 20 miles south (light monster activity, nothing organized).

Heading south toward [[Harren's Mill]], 60 miles out. Sprint pace ~30+ mph.

8:47 AM, 31 miles south — Toller spotted a goblin waypoint a quarter mile off the corridor: roughly 32 hostiles — goblins, hobgoblins, two worgs — with a loaded cart heading north toward the destroyed staging ground. Luther hung back; the group cleared the waypoint.

Kills: goblins (Level 71–134), hobgoblins (Level 131–173), worgs (Level 104–119). 32 total. Glass grenades and land-mine payloads tested on live targets — effective. Luther took assist credit. Luther and Wyn replaced spent grenades on-site (~40 seconds per grenade, with effectively unlimited mana). The group continued south.

### The Siege of Harren's Mill

11:14 AM — [[Toller Finch|Toller]] and [[Cademon Vale|Caedmon]] scouted ahead and reported a full siege: roughly 5,000 goblin variants encircling [[Harren's Mill]] — goblins L77–142, hobgoblins L131–173, brutes L169–201, orcs L176–195, ogres L214–221. The goblin force was drawing fresh troops from the first mana density ring just south of the town. Shamans provided ward coverage on the southeast. The stone walls held — the town had four [Mages] and a garrison of ~112 fighters.

The group ran the gauntlet. Luther carried [[Sable Mott|Sable]], [[Wyn Drace|Wyn]], and [[Pol Harren|Pol]]; Caedmon carried [[Bevan Roe|Bevan]]; Toller ran solo on his suppressed signature. Wyn threw up mobile glass screening. Luther carved a corridor through the northeast arc with [[Combustion]] (~40 kills, sprint and fire). They breached the northeast wall — Luther threw his passengers up, Caedmon and Toller jumped, Luther climbed. The gate opened once they were in.

Met garrison commander [[Edric Holm]] ([Knight] Level 38) and wall-watch captain [[Maren Ashell]] ([Sentinel] Level 31). The town had held four days; provisions for two more weeks. The southeast wall was under sustained shaman fire — eight goblin shamans running rotations behind a brute shield wall. The town's four [[Harren's Mill Mages|[Mages]]] (Carys, Tomas, Aldric, Fenn) kept the wards up but were exhausted.

Luther threw combustion charges from the battlement (seated, back-handed, blind over the wall) — ~12 kills, until the northeast line learned to stay out of throwing range. He fired an RPG at the southeast shaman ward; the ward absorbed most of it, but the blast killed two shamans and disrupted the brute shield wall.

Sable worked the healing house — the first [Cleric] the town had seen in years — clearing ~30 patients of burns, breaks, and lacerations with potent [[Solenne, Goddess of the Radiant Court|Solenne]]-domain healing. Luther discussed magic with her: channeled healing uses Solenne's domain pattern (the skill knows what "whole" looks like and pushes tissue toward it); Luther's magic is self-directed with no divine shaping.

### Dusk Sortie

Objectives: kill the shaman line, break the southeast pressure. Bevan mined the approach corridor. Edric committed 60 garrison fighters. The town [Mages] were tasked to hit the shaman ward from above; Wyn on the wall for damage; Pol holding the gate in Edric's formation.

Luther led out the gate and fired two directed shockwaves — compressing ambient air with micro-combustions and releasing it as concussive force. The first partially carved the front ranks and cracked the brute shield wall; the shaman ward collapsed under combined pressure from the wall [Mages] and the chaos below. The second broke the brute shield wall completely and devastated the exposed shaman line. Luther withdrew behind the walls as ordered.

He fired an RPG into the downed shamans — five of eight killed, three escaped deeper into the ring. Hobgoblins reformed around Caedmon in the smoke; Luther rocket-jumped off the battlement (combustion propulsion under his boots, first attempt, successful), killed four with rapid-fire snaps, and freed him. Caedmon disengaged clean.

Luther engaged the southeast ogre (Level 214) with an enhanced glass pistol round — half [[Mana Well]] propulsion, half Mana Well combustion payload — caving in its left chest and shoulder. The ogre stood back up; the wall defenders (four [Mages], archers, Wyn's glass) opened up and finished it (Wyn's glass through the neck and skull).

Edric's formation retreated in good order; Bevan detonated the mines behind them; the gate sealed. The southeast line broke — no shaman support — and the goblins pulled back half a mile, out of archer range now that the wards were gone. Losses: a forearm gash to Caedmon (treated by Sable), minor wounds to Luther ([[Slow Mend]]).

### Evening

Luther bathed and had his hair cut and beard regrown by [[Osser Brynn]] ([Trimmer] Level 29), paying with a low-grade healing potion (worth ~40 crowns) for a 2-copper haircut. He then dropped off the wall to eat the southeast field, consuming kills over ~4 hours (~47 bodies plus the ogre assist). Reached **Level 16**. Trait reinforcements: [[Unyielding Frame]] to moderate, [[Crushing Weight]] to early, [[Enhanced Magic]] to early, [[War Hide]] to early, [[Mana Saturation]] to early.
