Greenmonth 15th — Solo Encampment Assault

[[Luther Culvert|Luther]] went to the goblin encampment four miles southeast of [[Linne]] solo at approximately 1:00 AM on Greenmonth 15th. Approached the northern entrance and walked through the sentries. Assaulted the camp alone using [[Combustion]] and mace. Killed approximately 491 goblin variants. Roughly 100 escaped south. Consumed what he could — the rest were too charred to be worth eating. Reached Level 15.

[[Combat Bludgeoning]] leveled 8 to 9. [[Low Light Vision]] leveled 2 to 3. [[Combustion]] pressed against its Level 10 soft cap during the assault — no breakthrough yet.

Received class skill point. Selected [[Gorge]] — consumption of organic matter toward immediate physical restoration. Received general skill point.

Traits reinforced: [[Unyielding Frame]], [[War Hide]] (minimal to early), [[Tight Jaw]] (lockjaw reflex approaching threshold), [[Crushing Weight]], [[Loping Gait]].

---

### Morning — Aftermath

5:02 AM — [[Toller Finch|Toller]] woke [[Cademon Vale|Caedmon]]. Smoke to the southeast, Luther's pallet untouched. Caedmon, Toller, and [[Bevan Roe|Bevan]] approached the destroyed encampment and reached it at 6:11 AM. Found Luther on his back practicing [[Combustion]]. He reported 491 kills, ~100 escaped south.

Luther demonstrated a compressed air-burst projectile — combustion energy compressed and thrown as directed concussive force (an RPG variant) — and cratered a titan tree trunk with it. [[Combustion]] broke its Level 10 soft cap, 10 → 11. New capability: combustion energy compressed and projected as directed concussive force, detonating on contact or at a set distance.

Caedmon confronted Luther about going off solo. Luther didn't care. They agreed Luther would stay near Linne and practice for two days while the group used the time — Bevan reinforcing Linne's southern palisade, Pol drilling under Caedmon. (Caedmon has learned that Luther does what he thinks is best and will simply leave if pushed; he was annoyed, but let it go.)

### Day — Meditation and Ordnance

Luther spent his final general skill point on [[Meditation]]. Spent roughly six hours meditating. Meditation leveled 1 → 2 → 3 → 4 (soft cap at 5). Developed awareness of his internal mana flow — channels, pooling points, texture differences between mana in different regions of his body. Noticed Deeproot ambient mana entering through his outer tissue via [[Mana Saturation]] and [[Fey Mana Affinity]].

Shifted to Combustion practice. Attempted remote ignition — failed; mana disperses into the ambient Deeproot saturation past roughly 60 yards.

Developed grenade variants:

- **Impact grenades** — compressed mana core in a mana shell, detonates when the shell cracks on impact.
- **Delayed grenades** — internal fuse (a thin mana thread burning at ember intensity toward the core; a self-contained timer).
- **Frag grenades** — early sub-munition approach failed (mana components kept merging). Breakthrough: build each sub-munition as an independent grenade, cluster four around a weak central kick charge, wrap in a disposable skin. The kick scatters the sub-grenades; each detonates independently on its own fuse. Standardized at 4-cluster, ~3-of-4 reliable detonation. Wide-scatter (≈30 ft, anti-group) and tight-scatter (5–8 ft, anti-single-target) configurations.
- **Sequenced ordnance** — standard grenade to force movement, frag into the retreat path; RPG to breach, delayed frag through the hole.

[[Combustion]] leveled 11 → 12 → 13 → 14 overnight.
