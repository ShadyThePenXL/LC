## Group 16 — Caedmon's Group

**Leader:** [[Cademon Vale]] — [Hunter], Level 35. Former [Ranger], evolved at Level 14. Twenty years of field service in the [[Thornwall]] military. Scout Division.

**Original Composition (15 members):**

- [[Cademon Vale]] — [Hunter], Level 35
- [[Toller Finch]] — [Scout], Level 18
- [[Bevan Roe]] — [Trapper], Level 23
- Pell — class and level unrecorded. Killed in a goblin engagement during the first week.
- Dobs— class and level unrecorded. Killed in a goblin engagement during the first week.
- One unnamed member — killed alongside Pell and Dobs. Details lost.
- Nine additional members — killed or lost during the first two weeks of operations. Circumstances unrecorded.

By the time [[Luther Culvert]] arrived on Greenmonth 4th, only Caedmon, Toller, and Bevan remained. Bevan had a poisoned leg from a pitfall trap and could not walk properly.

---

### Operational History

**Deployment:** Group 16 entered [[The Deeproot]] from the northern tree line alongside all other groups. Assigned to the northern interior sector.

**First Two Weeks:** The group conducted patrols and attacked small goblin camps. They lost twelve members across multiple engagements and hazards. Caedmon pulled the survivors back to a concealed position he designated Vault Camp — a defensible location used as a base of operations.

**Greenmonth 4th:** [[Luther Culvert]] arrived in [[The Deeproot]] via divine transport from [[Andro, God of Borders and Passages|Andro]]. Caedmon encountered Luther after the latter cleared a goblin camp solo. They partnered — Luther would receive all corpses from the group's kills in exchange for fighting alongside them.

**Greenmonth 5th–10th:** Luther's presence changed the group's operational capacity. His consumption-based growth accelerated rapidly. The group destroyed multiple goblin camps, and Luther hunted independently — killing [[Blackshell Ant|blackshell ants]], [[Deeproot Stone Lizard|stone lizards]], [[Bugbear|bugbears]], [[Hobgoblin|hobgoblins]], [[Worg|worgs]], and a [[Deeproot Shadow Cat]]. Luther scouted the goblin staging ground alone and began harassing its perimeter.

**Greenmonth 11th:** The group traveled 100 miles northeast to a rendezvous point. Caedmon revealed there were no reinforcements — the rendezvous was a supply drop only. [[Trieht Blasterhubt]] appeared via projection and delivered supplies and the Mission Severance briefing: proceed 500 miles south to the center of [[The Deeproot]], locate and destroy a mana anomaly under the control of a drow [Sorcerer] named Veleth. No reinforcements. No rescue.

**Greenmonth 12th:** The group assaulted the goblin staging ground on two fronts. Luther struck the western side. Caedmon's team hit the east with trap corridors set by [[Bevan Roe|Bevan]] and [[Toller Finch|Toller]]. Luther freed three prisoners from other expedition groups — [[Sable Mott]] (Group 9), [[Wyn Drace]] (Group 9), and [[Pol Harren]] (Group 5). Luther killed a Fey [Witch] named Ryn and obtained intelligence about Veleth and a drow [Mage] named [[Thalrun Vel'Sharen|Thalrun]] (Level 47) who commands the staging ground.

**Greenmonth 13th:** The group assaulted the remaining camp forces head-on. [[Thalrun Vel'Sharen|Thalrun]] had returned and fought from the center with combustion magic. The group broke through the horde — [[Cademon Vale|Caedmon]] killed the ogres, Luther pushed through the flames and battered down Thalrun's wards using the Ward-Killer Shortsword, [[Dispel Chalk]], [[Null Sand]], and the counter-resonance bell. Luther killed Thalrun and consumed the entire camp. All members leveled. Three freed prisoners outfitted from [[Mission Severance Supply List|Mission Severance supplies]]. [[Bevan Roe|Bevan]]'s leg treated by [[Sable Mott|Sable]].

**Greenmonth 14th:** Group moving south through [[The Deeproot]] toward the center. Luther practicing [[Combustion]]. Encountered [[Deeproot Siphon Turtle|siphon turtles]]. Luther gained [[Mana Saturation]] and [[Mana Shell]] traits from consumption. Luther accepted a System Memory of [[Thalrun Vel'Sharen]]'s Combustion practice — [[Combustion]] leveled from 8 to 10. Group reached [[Linne]], a village under assault by roughly 300 goblin variants. Luther broke through the southern breach and destroyed the attacking force. Reached Level 14. Met [[Teysa Vael]] and [[Cael Harrick]], [[Linne]]'s prodigies.

**Greenmonth 15th:** Luther assaulted a goblin encampment four miles southeast of [[Linne]] solo at 1:00 AM. Killed approximately 491 goblin variants. Roughly 100 escaped south. Reached Level 15. Selected new class skill [[Gorge]]. In the morning [[Combustion]] broke its Level 10 cap during a concussive-projectile demonstration; Luther agreed to stay two days and practice while the group worked at Linne. He spent his general skill point on [[Meditation]] and developed combustion grenade designs. Combustion reached Level 14.

**Greenmonth 16th:** Luther and [[Wyn Drace]] engineered glass munitions at the practice camp — dual-layer-charge grenades, claymore charges, land-mine payloads, and crystalline-glass firearms (a pistol and a heavier variant, combustion-propelled). Combustion reached Level 15; Wyn's [[Summon Glass]] broke its soft cap. [[Bevan Roe|Bevan]] rebuilt Linne's southern palisade. [[Pol Harren|Pol]] drilled under Caedmon.

**Greenmonth 17th:** Group departed [[Linne]] south toward [[Harren's Mill]] (60 miles). Cleared a ~32-strong goblin waypoint 31 miles south. Continuing toward the center, aiding settlements along the way.
