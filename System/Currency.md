## Currency — Thornwall

Thornwall's coinage. Other kingdoms may use different currency.

- 10 Copper = 1 Silver
- 10 Silver = 1 Crown
- 10 Crown = 1 Scepter

So 1 Scepter = 10 Crown = 100 Silver = 1,000 Copper.

Reference values: a haircut runs about 2 copper; a low-grade healing potion is worth roughly 40 crowns.

Coin is tracked silently in play — it surfaces through natural consequence, not ledgers.
