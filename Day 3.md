Day 3 — Second Goblin Camp (Greenmonth 6th)

The group assaulted a larger [[Goblin|goblin]] camp. A Level 112 [[Goblin Brute|brute]] shattered [[Luther Culvert|Luther]]'s wrists. Luther dropped [[Iron Will]] to fight feral, eating mid-combat. [[Iron Skin]] failed. 

[[Cademon Vale|Caedmon]] finished the fight after entering melee range. Luther blamed Caedmon for a missed shot, straining their relationship. 

[[Iron Will]] evolved to become actively toggleable. 

Luther consumed two [[Goblin Brute|goblin brutes]] (Levels 112 and 108) and the camp, acquiring the [[Clotting]] trait from one and [[Stagger Shout]] skill from the other, and reaching Level 7. 

Luther ate deadfall branches from the ground to see if he could, gaining Intellect, then ate amberstone hardened tree sap from titan wood trees for significant Intellect and Knowledge growth as the sap is heavily mana infused. A creature called a [[Mana Warden|Warden]] arrived to eat sap forcing Luther to stop his feast. The warden killed a [[Rootstalker]].

Luther scavenged the corpse, gaining [[Dense Hide]] and reaching Level 9. Back at camp, Luther meditated with [[Apex Instinct]], leveling it from 3 to 4 to track ants. He killed a [[Blackshell Ant|blackshell ant]] and used [[Predator's Claim]], learning that [[Saturated Chitin]] overlapped with [[Dense Hide]]. And that he could also potentially get a Trait called [[Serrated Mandible]] if he consumed enough ants. 

Caedmon explained the difference between creatures, monsters, and classed beings. Luther meditated for five hours, leveling [[Apex Instinct]] to 5. Caedmon warned that ambient mana fuels detection skills in [[The Deeproot]], and mages are immensely powerful here.
