Trait [Unyielding Frame]

The bearer's bones, skin, and blood vessels reinforce. Blunt force is dispersed through increased bone density. Cutting and piercing injuries are resisted through skin hardening. Blood loss from open wounds is actively suppressed. The bearer's body adapts rapidly to repeated physical stress. Bone, skin, and circulatory tissue continue to strengthen through mana absorption, exertion, and trauma.

Combined from [[Iron Marrow]], [[Dense Hide]], and [[Clotting]].
