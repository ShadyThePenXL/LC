## The Deeproot Expedition

Twenty-five groups deployed simultaneously into [[The Deeproot]] by [[Thornwall]] Military Command under the authority of [[Trieht Blasterhubt]], [Archmage] of Strategic Command. Each group comprised roughly fifteen personnel — mixed classes, field-tested, drawn from the Ranger corps and attached specialists.

**Mission:** Penetrate the forest in a wide spread. Cull goblin concentrations. Provide aid to isolated villages and settlements within the tree line. Reestablish contact with communities cut off by the siege. Report on enemy strength and positioning.

**Deployment:** All twenty-five groups entered [[The Deeproot]] on the same day. The forest spans roughly 785,400 square miles. Groups were assigned sectors radiating outward from the northern tree line, each operating independently with scheduled rendezvous points for resupply.

**Current Status (as of Greenmonth 12th, Year 894):** Fifteen groups confirmed destroyed. Several unaccounted for. One group confirmed still operating.

---

### Group Reports

Divination records compiled from the Temple of Sight, Thornwall Capital. Scrying accuracy degrades significantly inside [[The Deeproot]] due to ambient mana interference. Many entries are fragmentary.

---

**Group 1** — Entered the northwestern sector. Led by a Level 29 [Warden]. Fourteen members including two [Priests] and a [Siege Engineer]. Reached their first village on day three — found it emptied, structures intact, no bodies. Pursued tracks south. Last scrying contact showed the group engaged with a large hobgoblin column. No survivors located.

**Group 2** — Assigned to the northern perimeter. Led by a Level 31 [Cavalier] on foot (mounts refused the tree line). Twelve members confirmed entering the forest; three turned back within the first day due to mana sickness. The remaining nine made contact with a goblin forward camp on day four and destroyed it. Pushed deeper. Lost scrying contact on day six. Presumed destroyed.

**Group 3** — Central-north sector. Led by a Level 26 [Bladesworn]. Fifteen members. Encountered a [[Mana Warden]] on day two. Four killed immediately. The remaining eleven scattered. Scrying located three survivors moving northeast six days later. Current status unknown.

**Group 4** — Northwestern interior. Led by a Level 33 [Bulwark]. Fourteen members including a Level 28 [Diviner] who maintained partial contact with Command longer than any other group. Cleared two goblin camps and reached a village called Edden's Hollow, still populated. Defended it for three days against probing attacks. The [Diviner]'s last message reported an organized assault of several hundred goblins supported by ogres. Contact severed. Village presumed fallen.

**Group 5** — Western interior. Led by a Level 27 [Pathfinder]. Fifteen members including [[Pol Harren]] ([Trapper], Level 14). Group fragmented after an ambush involving worgs on day five. Multiple members separated. Pol was captured and caged at the goblin staging ground. The [Pathfinder] and at least four others were last seen moving south under pursuit. Fates unknown.

**Group 6** — West-central sector. Led by a Level 30 [Sentinel]. Thirteen members. Made strong initial progress — cleared three camps in the first four days. Encountered an Elite bugbear pack on day six that killed their lead [Scout] and both [Trappers]. The group retreated north. Scrying showed seven survivors crossing back toward the tree line on day nine. Whether they made it out is unconfirmed.

**Group 7** — Central sector. Led by a Level 35 [Iron Vanguard]. Fifteen members including a Level 30 [Elementalist]. One of the strongest groups deployed. Pushed deep and fast. Last scrying contact placed them roughly 200 miles south of the tree line — further than any other group at that point. No contact since day seven. No bodies located by scrying.

**Group 8** — Central-east sector. Led by a Level 24 [Ranger]. Twelve members. Ambushed on day one by a goblin force that appeared to be waiting for them at the tree line. Eight killed in the first engagement. Four retreated. Two confirmed dead by day three. Two unaccounted for.

**Group 9** — Eastern interior. Led by a Level 28 [Huntsman]. Fifteen members including [[Sable Mott]] ([Scout], Level 21) and [[Wyn Drace]] ([Ranger], Level 19). The group was hit by a coordinated three-sided attack on day six — goblins, hobgoblins, and bugbears operating together. The [Huntsman] and six others confirmed dead. Sable, Wyn, and at least two others were captured. Sable and Wyn were caged at the goblin staging ground for eleven days before being freed by [[Luther Culvert]]. The fate of other captured members is unknown.

**Group 10** — Eastern sector. Led by a Level 26 [Warsinger]. Fourteen members. Reached a village called Mossbridge that was still holding. Assisted in defense for two days. A force of several hundred goblins with orc support overran the village on day eight. Scrying showed the village burning. Five group members were seen retreating east. No further contact.

**Group 11** — Southeastern sector. Led by a Level 31 [Shieldbearer]. Fifteen members. Cleared goblin presence around two settlements and established a temporary defensive line. Contact maintained through day ten. The [Shieldbearer] reported increasing pressure from the south — organized forces, not raiding parties. Last message indicated they were pulling the civilians north. Unaccounted for since.

**Group 12** — Southern approach. Led by a Level 29 [Stalker]. Thirteen members. Moved fast and quiet. Avoided large engagements. Scouted the southern interior and reported back the first confirmation of massive goblin concentrations deeper in the forest. Their intelligence contributed to what later became the Mission Severance briefing. Last contact on day eight. The [Stalker] indicated they had been identified and were being hunted. Presumed destroyed.

**Group 13** — South-central sector. Led by a Level 25 [Mender]. An unusual group — heavier on support classes, intended to reach and sustain isolated communities. Eleven combat-capable members. Reached a settlement called Root's Cradle. Provided medical aid and fortified the perimeter. Last scrying showed them still in the village on day nine, under siege but holding. Current status unknown.

**Group 14** — Southwestern sector. Led by a Level 32 [Reaver]. Fourteen members. Aggressive doctrine. Destroyed four goblin camps in the first five days and left a trail of burned ground. Pushed too deep too fast. Encountered what the [Diviner] in Group 4 described as "something that isn't goblins" near a mana-dense ravine. All contact lost on day six. No survivors located.

**Group 15** — Western perimeter. Led by a Level 27 [Archer]. Fifteen members. Held a section of the tree line and ran patrols outward. Methodical and cautious. Maintained contact with Command through day eleven — the second longest of any group. Reported steady increase in goblin activity moving northeast through their sector. Their final report noted a column of over a thousand goblins passing within two miles of their position. They held position and were not engaged. Contact lost the following day. Reason unknown.

**Group 16** — Northern interior. Led by [[Cademon Vale]] ([Hunter], Level 34). Originally fifteen members including [[Toller Finch]] ([Scout], Level 17), [[Bevan Roe]] ([Trapper], Level 22), and twelve others. Lost three members early — Pell, Harren, and a third unnamed — to goblin encounters and a pitfall trap. The remaining group was reduced further over the following days. By the time [[Luther Culvert]] arrived, only Caedmon, Toller, and Bevan remained. See [[Caedmon's Group]] for full detail.

**Group 17** — Northeastern sector. Led by a Level 28 [Sworn Shield]. Fourteen members. Engaged a hobgoblin-led force on day three and won decisively. Pushed east. Encountered a second, larger force on day five. Fought a running retreat for two days. Seven confirmed dead. The [Sworn Shield] and at least three others were last seen moving north. Unaccounted for.

**Group 18** — Far eastern sector. Led by a Level 30 [Beastcaller]. Fifteen members including two bonded war animals. Entered through the eastern tree line. Their sector was unusually quiet — almost no goblin presence. The [Beastcaller] reported the animals were agitated and refused to move south after day four. The group diverted west. Last contact on day seven showed them intact and moving. Current status unknown.

**Group 19** — Far western interior. Led by a Level 34 [Duskblade]. Thirteen members. One of the deeper-penetrating groups. Cleared a path through heavy goblin territory and reached what appeared to be an abandoned drow outpost on day nine. The [Duskblade] reported signs of recent ritual activity and structural damage inconsistent with goblin capability. Contact lost the same day. Whether they engaged something at the outpost or were struck from outside is unknown.

**Group 20** — Central-west sector. Led by a Level 26 [Farseer]. Fifteen members. The [Farseer] provided better-than-average scrying contact due to her class. She reported goblin movements, camp locations, and force compositions that informed Command's understanding of the invasion's scale. On day ten she reported a drow mage observing her group from a distance. She identified the drow's presence as significantly above anything they could engage. The group retreated. Last contact showed them moving northwest at speed. Whether they reached the perimeter is unconfirmed.

**Group 21** — Southern interior. Led by a Level 29 [Ironclad]. Fourteen members. Made steady progress south. Found and cleared a goblin supply line on day six. Burned three supply caches. On day eight, an organized counterattack hit them — goblins with orc and bugbear support, moving with a coordination that suggested classed leadership. Six killed. The [Ironclad] held a rearguard while the rest retreated. He was last scried standing alone in a clearing. The remaining eight were tracked north for two more days before scrying failed. Fate unknown.

**Group 22** — Southeastern interior. Led by a Level 31 [Wardkeeper]. Fifteen members including a Level 25 [Priest] of [[Solenne, Goddess of the Radiant Court|Solenne]]. Reached a mana-dense area where scrying degraded significantly. Fragmentary contact suggested they encountered something underground — possibly a root-level tunnel system used by goblins for movement. The [Priest] broadcast a single distress invocation on day seven. No follow-up. Presumed destroyed.

**Group 23** — Far southern sector. Led by a Level 33 [Vanguard]. Fourteen members. Deployed with the explicit objective of pushing as deep as possible. Alongside Group 7, one of the two groups intended to reach the deep interior. The [Vanguard] was experienced — thirty years of service, three class evolutions. His group maintained discipline and pace. Last contact on day eleven placed them approximately 300 miles south. The [Vanguard]'s final message was brief: "Forces here are not frontier-grade. Adjusting approach." No further contact.

**Group 24** — Eastern perimeter. Led by a Level 25 [Quartermaster]. A logistics group — intended to establish forward supply caches for other groups to use. Twelve combat personnel and three pack animals. They established two caches in the first three days. On day four, both caches were raided and destroyed. The goblins knew where they were. The [Quartermaster] reported the possibility of a classed intelligence directing enemy operations. He was correct. The group pulled back to the tree line. Whether they exited the forest is unconfirmed.

**Group 25** — Northeastern perimeter. Led by a Level 27 [Hearthguard]. Fifteen members. Tasked with establishing a fallback point inside the tree line for returning groups. They built a fortified position and held it for nine days. Three groups (6, 17, and 18) were expected to fall back through their position. None arrived. The [Hearthguard] held the position until day twelve, then withdrew north with all fifteen members intact. Current status: believed to have exited the forest. Unconfirmed by Command.
