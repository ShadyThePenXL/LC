
Alignment / Domain: God of Greed and Starvation (Unaligned)

Actions & Gifts: Embodiment of Gluttony. At war with [[Solenne, Goddess of the Radiant Court|Solenne]]'s court. Forcibly granted [[Unfathomable Gluttony]] and [[Ravenous Constitution]] to [[Luther Culvert]]. Issued the quest The Crowned Feast.

[[Unfathomable Gluttony]] — All consumed matter converts towards attribute growth. Hunger cannot be removed.

[[Ravenous Constitution]] — The body has been reshaped to serve endless consumption. Anything eaten is digested with extreme efficiency and converted toward physical growth. Sustenance once wasted is now fully extracted. Spoilage, toxins, and rawness in consumed material no longer cause harm.
